Projects

Herbert Simon – AI-Based Portfolio Management

Our project Herbert Simon is an AI-powered portfolio management system that integrates financial data, sentiment analysis, and reinforcement learning to optimize investment decisions. We became finalists in TEKNOFEST 2025 with this project, and I served as the team leader. We developed models using FinBERT for sentiment, LSTM for stock prediction, and reinforcement learning for dynamic portfolio rebalancing. The system aims to provide real-time, intelligent investment recommendations.
